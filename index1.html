Thank you for your interest in partnering with Book Trust!

For more than 20 years, Book Trust has partnered with hundreds of schools and districts across the United States to promote student book choice and ownership, while supporting teachers as they facilitate joyful reading experiences in their classrooms. Book Trust offers schools developmentally and culturally relevant print book options for Pre-K through Grade 5 students at a fraction of the cost to schools—and at no cost to students or their families—through a collaboration with the world’s largest publisher and distributor of children’s books. Learn more at booktrust.org.
